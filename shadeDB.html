Welcome to ShadeDB — a Database-as-a-Service engineered for precision, speed, and resilience.

Performance That Speaks for Itself

ShadeDB was built to eliminate latency bottlenecks. With an average query response time of 0.3ms and a memory footprint under 1KB, ShadeDB doesn’t just store data — it executes it with intent. This isn’t just speed. It’s computation redesigned.

Cross-Platform & IoT Ready

Whether you’re building robots, embedded systems, or large-scale services — ShadeDB integrates seamlessly. Designed for near 98% uptime, ensuring your data stays accurate, accessible, and secure across every device, everywhere.

Data Ownership Reimagined

ShadeDB gives you full control. We’re laying the foundation for user-driven data ownership — where your contributions matter and privacy is non-negotiable. No tracking. No hidden analytics. Just your data, your rules.

Distributed Computational Power

Traditional caching is outdated. ShadeDB introduces a virtual node system that temporarily stores encrypted data across distributed servers. Even if your main instance goes offline, your private node keeps your application alive and responsive.

Every Command. Accounted For.

Our Vertical Store Receipt System tracks every transaction — successful, failed, or rolled back — directly on your instance. It’s your personal audit trail, giving you transparency and total control.

Deploy in Minutes

No complex configuration. No manual tuning. ShadeDB sets up in minutes and optimizes itself dynamically. Its adaptive engine ensures seamless throughput, stable uptime, and unmatched reliability — so you can focus on building, not babysitting.
